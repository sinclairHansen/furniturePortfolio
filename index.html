About Me

I started redesigning furniture about 10 years ago, and I’ve loved the process of transforming vintage pieces and giving them new life. Whenever possible, I share a piece’s origin story so it can continue on through future generations. I’m drawn to unique furniture with character and history, and I enjoy helping clients choose custom colors and finishes that exceed expectations. If you’re interested in a custom piece, feel free to email me.
